Overview

India operates one of the world’s largest public food procurement systems. The government procures wheat (rabi), paddy/rice (kharif), and coarse grains at the Minimum Support Price (MSP) through the Food Corporation of India (FCI) and state government agencies. Procured stocks underpin the Public Distribution System (PDS), midday-meal schemes, and the National Food Security Act (NFSA) welfare programmes.

Key policy features: - Procurement is price-floor insurance for farmers — FCI and state agencies buy all offered quantities at MSP. - Stocks feed welfare: NFSA covers 80 crore beneficiaries with 5 kg/person/month of subsidised grain. - Buffer norms guide minimum stock requirements for food security.

Section 1 · Total Procurement by Crop

Code 
t1 <- fread("/home/pawan/procurement/procurement.csv")

## Summarise by crop and marketing season
proc_sum <- t1[, .(Total     = sum(Total, na.rm=TRUE),
                   FCI       = sum(FCI, na.rm=TRUE),
                   StateAgency = sum(StateAgency, na.rm=TRUE)),
               by = .(Crop, MarketingSeason)]

proc_sum[, FCI_Share   := round(FCI/Total*100, 1)]
proc_sum[, State_Share := round(StateAgency/Total*100, 1)]
proc_sum[, tt := paste0("<b>",Crop,"</b> | Season: ",MarketingSeason,
                         "<br>Total: ",round(Total,1)," LMT",
                         "<br>FCI: ",round(FCI,1)," (",FCI_Share,"%)",
                         "<br>States: ",round(StateAgency,1)," (",State_Share,"%)")]

## By-crop faceted bar
p1 <- ggplot(proc_sum, aes(x=MarketingSeason, y=Total, fill=Crop)) +
  geom_bar_interactive(aes(tooltip=tt, data_id=paste(Crop,MarketingSeason)),
                        stat="identity", width=0.75, alpha=0.9) +
  facet_wrap(~Crop, scales="free_y") +
  scale_fill_brewer(palette="Set2") +
  scale_y_continuous(labels=comma_format()) +
  my_theme + theme(legend.position="none") +
  labs(title="Foodgrain Procurement by Crop & Marketing Season",
       subtitle="Hover over bars for FCI vs state agency breakdown",
       y="Quantity (Lakh Metric Tonnes)", x="Marketing Season",
       caption="Source: DFPD Procurement Portal | Analysis: Pawan")

girafe(ggobj=p1, width_svg=13, height_svg=7,
       options=list(
         opts_tooltip(css="background:#fff;color:#333;padding:10px;border-radius:6px;border:1px solid #ddd;font-size:12px;"),
         opts_hover(css="opacity:0.9;stroke:#333;stroke-width:1.5;"),
         opts_hover_inv(css="opacity:0.3;"),
         opts_zoom(min=0.8, max=3),
         opts_sizing(rescale=TRUE, width=1)))
